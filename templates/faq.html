FAQ



Q. What is Farmers' Portal?

A. The Farmers' Portal is a web portal to connect farmers and experts. Experts can help farmers deal with various agricultural problems.


Q. How to start using this portal?

A. Experts and Farmers can both register themselves using the sign up form. After signing up, you may login and start working.


Q. What facilities are available in this portal for a farmer?

A. A farmer can register his/her crop for maintenance. He can search for other farmers in his/her location. Most importantly, he/she can post a question to experts regarding farming. Also, if farmer is facing a crop disease, he/she can take expert's advice. Many more options available.


Q. What can an expert expect in this site?

A. An Expert, after successful registration, can study various crops/infected crops in any location. He may study the crops grown by other farmers and help them accordingly. He may also study weather info for some farmer's location.


Q. I am a farmer and my crop is infected. What should I do??

A. Simply make a new post about this. During making a new post, ensure that you upload a good photo describing the infection in the crop. After the post is created, an expert will respond accordingly.
